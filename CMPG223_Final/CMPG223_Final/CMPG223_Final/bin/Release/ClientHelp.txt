Quotes Tab
When you enter this tab, you will need to fill in your car details and
choose a service type. You need to press the add button to add a service
type. A service type can also be removed if you select the service type
to remove and click on the remove button. The added service types will
be displayed in the listbox below the buttons. You can then press 
generate quote when you are finished, then there will display a popup
message that will ask if your vehicle can be added to the database.
You can then press the button to approve the quote. You can also email
the quote to you by pressing on the email quote button.

Pay for Service Tab
Under this tab you will be required to fill in your card details. When
you are finished you can press the process payment button, the program
will then email to confirm the transaction.
